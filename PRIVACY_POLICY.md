# Privacy Policy for BrainyArcade

**Effective Date:** October 2023

## 1. Introduction
BrainyArcade is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our mobile, tablet, and TV applications.

## 2. Data Collection
- **Personal Information**: We do **not** collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
- **Game Progress**: High scores and game progress are stored locally on your device using a Room database.
- **Analytics**: We use **Firebase Analytics** to collect anonymous usage data (e.g., which games are played most often) to improve the app experience.
- **Crash Reporting**: We use **Firebase Crashlytics** to receive technical reports on app failures to ensure stability.

## 3. Advertising
We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data according to its own privacy policy to provide relevant ads.

## 4. Children's Privacy
BrainyArcade does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Our games are logic puzzles suitable for all ages.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions, please contact us via the GitHub repository where this project is hosted.
